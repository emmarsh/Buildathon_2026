The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted NDRF team, on their 12th Raising Day celebrations.
“Greetings to NDRF team on their 12th Raising Day celebrations.
NDRF’s efforts in disaster response, rescue and relief are commendable.
The NDRF is rightly focussing on strengthening systems, processes and capacity building, which help in minimising loss of life or property”, the Prime Minister said.

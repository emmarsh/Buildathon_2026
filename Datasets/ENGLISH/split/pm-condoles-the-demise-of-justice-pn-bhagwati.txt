The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Justice PN Bhagwati.
“The demise of Justice PN Bhagwati is saddening.
He was a stalwart of India’s legal fraternity.
My deepest condolences.
Justice PN Bhagwati’s remarkable contributions made our judicial system more accessible and gave voice to millions “, the Prime Minister said.

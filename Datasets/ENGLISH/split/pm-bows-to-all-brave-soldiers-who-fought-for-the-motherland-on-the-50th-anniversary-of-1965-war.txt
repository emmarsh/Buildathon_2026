PM bows to all brave soldiers who fought for the Motherland, on the 50th anniversary of 1965 war
Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has bowed to all brave soldiers who fought for the Motherland, on the 50th anniversary of 1965 war.
“As we mark the 50th anniversary of 1965 war, I bow to all brave soldiers who fought for our Motherland in the war.
The courage and bravery of our Armed Forces is very inspiring.
They triumphed over every obstacle and protected India’s unity and integrity.
We recall the dynamic leadership of Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri.
In 1965 he led from the front and was a key source of strength for the Nation”, the Prime Minister.

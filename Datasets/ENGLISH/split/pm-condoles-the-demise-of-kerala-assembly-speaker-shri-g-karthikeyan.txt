Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of Kerala Assembly Speaker, Shri G. Karthikeyan.
“My condolences to the family and well-wishers of Kerala Assembly Speaker, Shri G. Karthikeyan on his demise.
May his soul rest in peace.
Shri G. Karthikeyan will be remembered as a grassroots leader who dedicated his life to serving the people of Kerala”, the Prime Minister said.

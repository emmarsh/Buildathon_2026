Mr. Masayoshi Son, Chairman and CEO, Soft Bank Corporation, Shri Sunil Bharti Mittal, Chairman, Bharti Enterprises, and Mr. Tadashi Maeda, Senior MD of the Japan Bank for International Cooperation, today called on the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi.
They expressed keenness to invest in the renewable energy sector in India as part of the “Make in India” initiative.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has expressed sadness over the killing of CRPF personnel in Sukma, Chhattisgarh.
“Saddened by the killing of CRPF personnel in Sukma.
Tributes to the martyrs and condolences to their families.
May the injured recover quickly.
Spoke to Home Minister Rajnath Singh Ji on the situation in Sukma.
He is going to Sukma to take stock of the situation”, the Prime Minister said.

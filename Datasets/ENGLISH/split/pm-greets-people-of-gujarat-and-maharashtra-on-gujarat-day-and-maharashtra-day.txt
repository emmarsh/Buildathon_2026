Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people of Gujarat and Maharashtra on Gujarat Day and Maharashtra Day, respectively.
Text of the Prime Minister’s message to the people of Maharashtra:
My dear Sisters and Brothers of Maharashtra,
My greetings to you on the occasion of Maharashtra Day!
Maharashtra is a land that has contributed immensely to the development of the Nation.
It is a land of thinkers, saints and it is the land of the brave and the courageous.
The hardworking nature of the people of Maharashtra has always been well-known.
My best wishes for the development journey of Maharashtra.
Text of the Prime Minister’s message to the people of Gujarat:
Dear friends,
Gujarat Diwas greetings to everyone.
I pray that Gujarat, a land that is extremely dear to us, scales newer heights of progress in the years to come.
There is something unique about the soil of Gujarat!
The warm nature and the enthusiastic spirit of the people has benefitted the State immensely.
Once again, my best wishes on Gujarat Day.

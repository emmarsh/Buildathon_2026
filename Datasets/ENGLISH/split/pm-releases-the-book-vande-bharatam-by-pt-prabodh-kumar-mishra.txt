Prime Mnister, Shri Narendra Modi, today released the book “Vande Bharatam”, written by Pandit Prabodh Kumar Mishra.
The book is an anthology of Nationalistic Poems in Sanskrit, which were published in different newspapers of Odisha on various occasions.
Shri Dharmendra Pradhan, Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Petroleum and Natural Gas, and Shri Bhartruhari Mehtab, Member of Parliament, were also present on the occasion.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today released a biography of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel in Braille.
This has been prepared by Dr. A Saibaba Goud, for the benefit of the visually challenged.
Dr Goud also runs the Devnar School for the Blind, in Hyderabad.
Shri Bandaru Dattatreya, Member of Parliament, was present on the occasion.

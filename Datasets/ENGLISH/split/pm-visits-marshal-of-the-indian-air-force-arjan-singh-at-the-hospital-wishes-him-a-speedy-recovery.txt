The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has visited R&R Hospital to see Marshal of the Indian Air Force Arjan Singh, who is critically ill.
The Prime Minister also met his family members and wished him a speedy recovery.
“Went to R&R Hospital to see Marshal of the Indian Air Force Arjan Singh, who is critically ill.
I also met his family members.
We are all praying for the speedy recovery of Marshal of the Indian Air Force Arjan Singh.
Doctors are doing their best”, the Prime Minister tweeted.

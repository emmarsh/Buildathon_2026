Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has spoken to Afghanistan President, Mohammad Ashraf Ghani.
“Spoke to President Aashraf Ghani and offered condolences on loss of lives due to the Kabul attack.
We are one when it comes to fighting terror.
President Aashraf Ghani expressed sadness on the unfortunate demise of Indian citizens in the attack in Kabul”, the Prime Minister said.

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has paid tribute to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar on his Mahaparinirvana Diwas.
“On his Mahaparinirvana Diwas, I bow to the venerable Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar.
His contribution to the Nation is eternal & invaluable.
Dr. Ambedkar was a man ahead of his time.
We remember his efforts to eradicate social evils and the importance he gave to education.
Babasaheb became a voice for the oppressed and downtrodden.
His ideals and thoughts continue to guide us in our quest for an equal society,” said the Prime Minister.

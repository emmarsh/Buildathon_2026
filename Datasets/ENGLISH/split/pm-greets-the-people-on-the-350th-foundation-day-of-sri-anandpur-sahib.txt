Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people on the 350th Foundation Day of Sri Anandpur Sahib.
“Greetings on the 350th Foundation Day of Sri Anandpur Sahib.
This is truly a special & historical occasion.
We remain inspired by the tenets of Sikhism & bow to the great Sikh Gurus, who have left a very deep impact on our culture & society,” the Prime Minister said.

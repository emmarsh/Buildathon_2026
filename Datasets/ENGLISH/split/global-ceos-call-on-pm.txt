A group of CEOs including Shri Anshu Jain of Deutsche Bank AG, Dr. Dieter Zetsche of Daimler AG, Dr. Kurt Bock of BASF, Dr. Heinrich Biersinger of Thyssen Krupp, Mr. Yngve Slyngstad of Norgos, and Dr. Feffrey Jaensubhakiji of GIC AM; called on the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi today.
They appreciated the economic reform initiatives of the Prime Minister, and also expressed their intention to increase investments in India.

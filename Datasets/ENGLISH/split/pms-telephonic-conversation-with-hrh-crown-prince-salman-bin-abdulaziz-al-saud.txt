The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today had a telephonic conversation with His Royal Highness Crown Prince Salman, of Saudi Arabia.
The Prime Minister enquired about the health of His Majesty, King Abdullah bin Abdulaziz Al Saud.
The Prime Minister conveyed his best wishes for the speedy and complete recovery of King Abdullah.
He also conveyed his best wishes to Crown Prince Salman for the discharge of his current duties and national responsibilities.

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the nation on the auspicious occasion of Bhai Dooj.
“भाई दूज के पावन पर्व की सभी देशवासियों को बहुत-बहुत बधाई।
Greetings to you all on the special occasion of Bhai Dooj, a festival that celebrates the strong brother-sister bond”, the Prime Minister said.

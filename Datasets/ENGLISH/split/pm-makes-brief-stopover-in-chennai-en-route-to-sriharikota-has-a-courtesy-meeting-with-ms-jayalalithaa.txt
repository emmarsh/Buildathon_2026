The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, made a brief stopover in Chennai this afternoon, on the way to Sriharikota.
He was received by the Governor of Tamil Nadu, Shri K. Rosaiah, and the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, Ms. J. Jayalalithaa.
The Prime Minister later had a courtesy meeting with Ms. Jayalalithaa, before leaving for Sriharikota.

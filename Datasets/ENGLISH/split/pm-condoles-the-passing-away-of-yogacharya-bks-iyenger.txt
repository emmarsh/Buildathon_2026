The Prime Minister has condoled the passing away of Yogacharya BKS Iyenger.
“I am deeply saddened to know about Yogacharya BKS Iyengar`s demise and offer my condolences to his followers all over the world.
Generations will remember Shri BKS Iyengar as a fine Guru, scholar and a stalwart who brought Yoga into the lives of many across the world,” the Prime Minister said.

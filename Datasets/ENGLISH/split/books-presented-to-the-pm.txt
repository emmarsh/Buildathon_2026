The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi was today presented with two books.
Dr. Tamilisai Soundararajan presented a book written by her in Tamil language on Shri Narendra Modi.
The title of the book is “Suvai Migu Theneer Thuligal” – Sweet Drops of Tea.
Shri V. Ponraj, presented a book co-authored by him with Former President APJ Abdul Kalam, titled “A Manifesto for Change.”

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Djibouti, on Djibouti’s Independence Day.
“On Djibouti’s Independence Day, I convey my greetings and best wishes to the people of Djibouti.
India truly cherishes our ties with Djibouti.
We remain grateful to Djibouti for the strong support and active cooperation during Yemen evacuation.
It indicates the robustness of our ties”, the Prime Minister said.

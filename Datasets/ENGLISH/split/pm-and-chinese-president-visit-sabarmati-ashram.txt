The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, and the Chinese President, Xi Jinping, today visited the Sabarmati Ashram in Ahmedabad.
President Jinping was dressed in an off-white Khadi jacket, which had been presented to him earlier today by the Prime Minister.
The two leaders spent a few minutes at Hriday Kunj in the Ashram, where President Jinping operated the charkha. (spinning wheel)

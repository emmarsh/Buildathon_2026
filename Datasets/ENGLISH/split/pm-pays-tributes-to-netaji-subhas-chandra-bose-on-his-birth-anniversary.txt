Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose, on his birth anniversary.
“His bravery, courage and patriotic zeal inspire us.
On his birth anniversary, I bow to the proud son of India, Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose.
Subhas Babu’s organisation and leadership skills were exceptional.
He was a phenomenal personality, for whom good of the Nation was paramount.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the loss of lives in the fire in a GAIL pipeline in Andhra Pradesh today.
He has spoken to the Petroleum Minister, Cabinet Secretary and GAIL Chairman and asked them to ensure immediate relief at the accident site.
Following is the text of the Prime Minister’s tweets:
“My thoughts with the families of those who lost their lives in the GAIL Pipeline fire in AP.
Prayers with the injured. ‎
I have spoken to the Petroleum Minister, Cabinet Secretary & GAIL Chairman and asked them to ensure immediate relief at the accident site.”

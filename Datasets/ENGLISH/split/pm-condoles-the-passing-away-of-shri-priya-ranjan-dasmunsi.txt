Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of former Union Minister, Shri Priya Ranjan Dasmunsi.
“Shri Priya Ranjan Dasmunsi was a popular leader with rich political and administrative experience.
He did notable work to popularise football in India.
Saddened by his demise.
My thoughts are with Deepa Dasmunsi ji and family as well as his supporters”, the Prime Minister said.

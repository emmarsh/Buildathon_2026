Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of Telangana and congratulated the state`s first Chief Minister, Shri K. Chandrasekhar Rao, on the creation of the new state of Telangana.
The Prime Minister has tweeted as follows:
“India gets a new state!
We welcome Telangana as our 29th state.
Telangana will add strength to our development journey in the coming years.
Telangana`s birth comes after years of struggle and sacrifices by several people.
We pay our respects to them today.
Congrats to K Chandrasekhar Rao Garu on taking oath as Telangana`s 1st CM.
My best wishes to people for the state`s development journey.
Centre assures complete support to the people & Government of Telangana in taking the state to newer heights of progress.”

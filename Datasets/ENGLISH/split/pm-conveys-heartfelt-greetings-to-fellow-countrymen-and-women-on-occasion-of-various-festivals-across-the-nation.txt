Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has conveyed heartfelt greetings to fellow countrymen and women on occasion of various festivals across the Nation.
“I convey my heartfelt greetings to our fellow countrymen and women on the various festivals across the Nation.
The festive spirit across India is gladdening.
It celebrates the diversity of India that we cherish and are extremely proud of.
आप सभी को मकर संक्रांति की हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं!
Wishing everyone on the auspicious occasion of Makar Sankranti.
Greetings on Uttarayan.
The sight of the skies full of colourful kites is a delight.
Have a wonderful day with family and friends!
ઉત્તરાયણ નિમિત્તે શુભેચ્છાઓ। રંગબેરંગી પતંગોથી અચ્ચાદિત આકાશ નયનરમ્ય છે. પરિવાર જનો અને મિત્રો સાથે અદ્ભુત દિવસ વિતાવો.
Pongal greetings to friends in TN!
Best wishes to our hardworking farmers for a great harvest.
May it bring more prosperity in their lives.
தமிழக மக்களுக்கு இனிய பொங்கல் நல்வாழ்த்துக்கள். கடின உழைப்பை வித்திட்ட விவசாயிகளுக்கு சிறப்பான அறுவடையும் வளமும் கிடைக்க வாழ்த்துகிறேன்.
My best wishes on Bhogi!
May this special day bring happiness to all and usher in a brighter and better tomorrow, full of unprecedented joy.
అందరికీ భోగి పండగ శుభాకాంక్షలు. ఈ పండగ అందరికీ సుఖసంతోషాలు ఇవ్వాలని ఆకాంక్షిస్తున్నా. ఆనందమయమైన రేపటికి ఇది నాంది కావాలి.
On the auspicious occasion of Magh Bihu, my greetings to the people of Assam.
May this festival add to the sprit of happiness and well-being.
মাঘ বিহুৰ শুভক্ষণত সমূহ অসমবাসী ৰাইজলৈ শুভেচ্ছা জ্ঞাপন কৰিলো। এই উত্সৱে আটাইৰে মাজত সুখ, শান্তি আৰু সমৃদ্ধি কঢ়িয়াই আনক”, the Prime Minister said.

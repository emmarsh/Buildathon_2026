Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated men and women Kabaddi teams for winning Kabaddi World Cups.
“Great news!
Our men and women Kabaddi teams win Kabaddi World Cups.
My congratulations to the sportspersons.
We are very proud.
This is the fifth consecutive win for the men’s team and fourth consecutive win for the women’s team.
Truly phenomenal performance” said the Prime Minister.

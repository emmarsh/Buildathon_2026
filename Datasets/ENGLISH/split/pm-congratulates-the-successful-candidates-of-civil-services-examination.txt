PM congratulates the successful candidates of Civil Services Examination
The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated the successful candidates of Civil Services Examination.
“Congratulations to all those who successfully cleared the Civil Services Exam and my best wishes as you begin the journey to serve the Nation.
To those who didn’t clear the exam- such moments are a part of life.
This must not deter you from your future endeavours.
My best wishes”, the Prime Minister said.

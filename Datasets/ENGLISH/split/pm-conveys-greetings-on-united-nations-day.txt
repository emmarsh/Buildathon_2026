Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has conveyed his greetings on the occasion of the United Nations Day.
“I convey my greetings on @UN Day.
It is a day to celebrate the achievements of the UN & look at a roadmap for the future….
Need of the hour is for all @UN member nations to work together & further the cause of world peace & prosperity in the years to come” Shri Narendra Modi said in his tweets.

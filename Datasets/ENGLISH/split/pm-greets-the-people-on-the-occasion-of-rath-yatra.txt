Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the people on the occasion of the Rath Yatra.
The Prime Minister’s Message:-
“Wishing everyone on the auspicious occasion of the Rath Yatra.
Jai Jagannath.”
The Prime Minister also uploaded some photographs of the Rath Yatra of 2013 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, with the message:-
“Rath Yatra brings back many memories from Gujarat.
Here are photos from the Rath Yatra of 2013 in Ahmedabad:”

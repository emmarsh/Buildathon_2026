Prime Minister designate Narendra Modi expressed immense pride & joy that Gujarat was getting its 1st woman Chief Minister in Smt.
Anandiben Patel.
While conveying his best wishes to her on the occasion of taking over the Chief Ministership of Gujarat, Shri Modi said that Anandiben had rich experience in Administration.
Shri Modi also expressed his hope that Gujarat would scale new heights in the years to come.

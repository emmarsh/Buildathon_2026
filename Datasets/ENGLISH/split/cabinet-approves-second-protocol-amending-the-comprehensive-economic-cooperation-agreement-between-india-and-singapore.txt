The Union Cabinet chaired by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has given its ex-post facto approval to the Second Protocol amending the Comprehensive Economic Cooperation Agreement (CECA) between India and Singapore.
The CECA was signed on 24th August 2018.
Benefits: Signing of the Second Protocol will enhance bilateral trade and will deepen the Economic Cooperation between India and Singapore.
It will also improve utilization of CECA.

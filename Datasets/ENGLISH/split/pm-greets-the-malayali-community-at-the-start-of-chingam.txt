Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted the Malayali community at the start of Chingam, the first month of the Malayalam New Year.
“Greetings to the Malayali community at the start of Chingam, the first month of the Malayalam New Year.
May this year bring happiness and prosperity.
Glad to mark this special day of Malayali community in UAE, where the community has a key presence and is admired for its industrious nature,” the Prime Minister said.

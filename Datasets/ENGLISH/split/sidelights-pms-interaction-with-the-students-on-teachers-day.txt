The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi was in great demand when he went among the children and mingled with them freely after the function.
Students from all corners of the auditorium called for him and the hall was full of ‘sir come here’ calls.
The Prime Minister obliged smilingly.
Children were thrilled to have him amongst them.
The Prime Minister presented books to students who spoke about Dr S. Radhakrishnan.
One of the questioner was differently abled, she received special encouragement from the Prime Minister.
The Prime Minister also released a brochure named – “Swachh Bharat; Swacch Vidyalaya”.
The brochure contains an appeal for the Swachh Bharat; Swachh Vidyalaya Abhiyaan, calling upon the corporate sector and philanthropists to contribute liberally for this important national cause.

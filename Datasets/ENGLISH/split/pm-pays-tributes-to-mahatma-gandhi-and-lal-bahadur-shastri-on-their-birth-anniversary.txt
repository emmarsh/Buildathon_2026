Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi paid tributes to Mahatma Gandhi and Lal Bahadur Shastri on their birth anniversary.
“गांधी जयंती पर बापू को शत्-शत् नमन!
I bow to beloved Bapu on Gandhi Jayanti.
His noble ideals motivate millions across the world.
जवानों एवं किसानों के प्रणेता एवं देश को कुशल नेतृत्व प्रदान करने वाले शास्त्री जी को नमन!
Remembering Lal Bahadur Shastri ji on his Jayanti”, the Prime Minister said.

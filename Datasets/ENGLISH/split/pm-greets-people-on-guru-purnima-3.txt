The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted people on the occasion of Guru Purnima.
“ गुरु पूर्णिमा के पावन अवसर पर देशवासियों को हार्दिक शुभकामनाएं।
Guru Purnima greetings to everyone.
Ours is a society that holds teachers in very high esteem.
We thank our teachers, who always inspire and bring out the best in us”, the Prime Minister said.

Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi spoke on telephone to Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina of Bangladesh, Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif of Pakistan and President Dr. Ashraf Ghani.
He conveyed the good wishes for the holy month of Ramadan to them and their people.
He expressed hope for peace, tranquility and harmony in their countries and in the region during this month of piety and prayers.
He thanked Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina for his successful visit to Bangladesh and the extraordinary hospitality, friendship and goodwill that he received from the people and government of Bangladesh.
He expressed confidence that the visit has set the stage for a new era in bilateral cooperation and regional partnership between the two countries.
He reiterated to Prime Minister Sharif his message of peaceful, friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries.

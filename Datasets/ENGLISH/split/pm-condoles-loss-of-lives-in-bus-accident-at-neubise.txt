Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has condoled the loss of lives in the bus accident at Neubise.
“Very saddening news from Nepal.
My thoughts are with the families of those who lost their lives in the bus accident at Neubise.
A Team from our Embassy in Nepal reached the spot within an hour of the accident to oversee rescue & relief operations.
Embassy officials are present at every hospital where the injured are being treated & Embassy is taking care of the treatment,” the Prime Minister said.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has condoled the passing away of educationist Shri Kireet Joshi.
“Saddened by demise of Shri Kireet Joshi.
He will be remembered as a fine scholar and educationist, devoted to the principles of Sri Aurobindo.
As advisor to Gujarat CM, Kireet Bhai played a key role in setting up of Children`s University and Institute of Teacher Education,” the Prime Minister said.

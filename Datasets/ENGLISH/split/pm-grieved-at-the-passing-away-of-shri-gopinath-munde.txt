The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the untimely passing away of the Union Minister for Rural Development, Shri Gopinath Munde, in a road accident this morning.
Expressing his sadness and shock, the Prime Minister described Shri Gopinath Munde as a true mass leader, who rose to great heights and tirelessly served people.
Following is the text of the Prime Minister’s tweets:
“Extremely saddened & shocked by the demise of my friend & colleague Gopinath Munde ji.
His demise is a major loss for the Nation & the Govt.
Gopinath Munde ji was a true mass leader.
Hailing from backward sections of society, he rose to great heights & tirelessly served people.
My tributes to a dynamic leader whose premature demise leaves a void hard to fill.
Condolences to Munde ji’s family.
We stand by them in this hour of grief.”

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of former civil servant Shri Naresh Chandra.
“Shri Naresh Chandra was an outstanding public servant, who left an indelible mark on matters of governance and policy.
Pained by his demise.
I remember an insightful discussion with Naresh Ji, who as USA ambassador invited me for dinner.
He believed in strong India-USA friendship.” the Prime Minister said.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Justice Dalveer Bhandari on being re-elected to the International Court of Justice.
“I congratulate Justice Dalveer Bhandari on being re-elected to the International Court of Justice.
His re-election is a proud moment for us.
Congratulations to External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj and her entire team at Ministry of External Affairs and diplomatic missions for their untiring efforts that have led to India’s re-election to ICJ.
Our deep gratitude to all the members of UNGA as well as UNSC for their support and trust in India”, the Prime Minister said.

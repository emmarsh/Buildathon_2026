Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of former Kerala and Bihar Governor, Shri R S Gavai.
“Shri RS Gavai was a very experienced public figure, serving in the Maharashtra Legislative Council and as Governor of Bihar and Kerala.
We will always remember Shri R S Gavai’s contribution towards welfare of poor and marginalised.
My condolences to his family on his demise”, the Prime Minister said.

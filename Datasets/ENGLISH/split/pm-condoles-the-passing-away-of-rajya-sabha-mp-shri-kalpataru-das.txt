Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the passing away of Rajya Sabha MP Shri Kalpataru Das.
“Pained to hear about the passing away of Rajya Sabha MP Shri Kalpataru Das.
My condolences to his family.
May his soul rest in peace.
Shri Kalpataru Das was actively involved in social work and was extremely passionate about Odisha’s progress”, the Prime Minister said.

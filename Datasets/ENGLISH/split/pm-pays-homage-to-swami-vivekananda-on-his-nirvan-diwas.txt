The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid homage to Swami Vivekananda on his Nirvan Diwas.
The Prime Minister tweeted:
“I bow to Swami Vivekananda on his Nirvan Diwas.
His thoughts & ideals remain an inspiration for everyone devoted to serving our nation.
Swami Vivekananda conquered the entire world with his life & message.
Best tribute to him would be to imbibe his ideals in our daily lives.”

On the occasion of the Nobel Peace Prize ceremony in Oslo, Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, congratulated the award winners, Kailash Satyarthi and Malala Yousafzai.
“The entire nation watches the ceremony in Oslo with great joy and immense pride.
Congratulations Kailash Satyarthi!
I also congratulate the young Malala Yousafzai for the momentous achievement,” the Prime Minister said.

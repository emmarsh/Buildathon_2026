Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has expressed grief over the news of bus accident in Udhampur, Jammu and Kashmir.
“Disturbing news from Jammu and Kashmir.
Bus accident in Udhampur has saddened me.
My thoughts are with families of the deceased.
Prayers with the injured.
I have asked my ministerial colleague Dr. Jitendra Singh ji to stay in touch with authorities and ensure timely assistance”, the Prime Minister said.

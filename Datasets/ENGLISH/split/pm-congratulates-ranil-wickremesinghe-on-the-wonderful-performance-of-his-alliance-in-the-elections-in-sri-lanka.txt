Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated Mr. Ranil Wickremesinghe, on the wonderful performance of his alliance in the elections in Sri Lanka.
“Spoke to Mr. Ranil Wickremesinghe and congratulated him on his alliance’s wonderful performance in the elections.
I am confident that under Mr. Wickremesinghe’s leadership, bilateral ties between India and Sri Lanka will get stronger.
I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the people of Sri Lanka on the peaceful conduct of polls”, the Prime Minister said.

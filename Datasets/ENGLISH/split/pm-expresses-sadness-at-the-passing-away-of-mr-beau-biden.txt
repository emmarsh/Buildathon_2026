Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has expressed sadness at the passing away of Mr. Beau Biden.
“Saddened to learn about the passing away of Beau Biden, the eldest son of US Vice-President Joseph Biden, at a young age.
Apart from a deep loss to the family, a promising public career has been tragically cut short.
Our deepest condolences to the Biden family,” the Prime Minister said.

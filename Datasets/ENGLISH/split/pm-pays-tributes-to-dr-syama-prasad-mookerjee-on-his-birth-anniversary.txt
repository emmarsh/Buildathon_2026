The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid tributes to Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee on his birth anniversary.
The Prime Minister tweeted:
“Tributes to our inspiration, Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee on his birth anniversary.
His contribution to our Nation is invaluable.
If there was something that mattered most to Dr. Mookerjee it was serving the people.
He always spoke about education for the youth.
Let us devote ourselves to fulfilling Dr. Syama Prasad Mookerjee`s ideals.”

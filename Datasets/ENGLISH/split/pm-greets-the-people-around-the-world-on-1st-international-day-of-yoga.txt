PM greets the people around the world, on first International Day of Yoga
Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people around the world, on 1st International Day of Yoga.
“Greetings to people around the world on 1st International Day of Yoga!
Lets pledge to make Yoga an integral part of our daily lives
In some parts of the world, the 1st rays of the sun have already been welcomed by people practicing Yoga.This will continue across the world”, the Prime Minister said.

Prime Minister of Israel, Mr. Benjamin Netanyahu called Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi today to convey his “deepest appreciation and gratitude” Prime Minister for India’s “exemplary” support and assistance in the evacuation of Israeli citizens from Nepal.
Prime Minister Modi assured him of full support in the evacuation of other Israeli citizens from Nepal, if required.
The two leaders also briefly discussed bilateral relations.
Prime Minister Netanyahu expressed Israel’s interest in participating in Prime Minister’s “Make in India” mission.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today unveiled the plaque for laying of the Foundation Stone of the Fourth Container Terminal of the Jawaharlal Nehru Port at Mumbai.
The Governor of Maharashtra Shri Ch.
Vidyasagar Rao, the Chief Minister of Maharashtra Shri DevendraFadnavis, and the Union Minister for Shipping Shri Nitin Gadkari were present on the occasion.

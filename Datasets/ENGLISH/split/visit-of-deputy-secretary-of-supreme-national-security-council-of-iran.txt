Deputy Secretary of the Supreme National Security Council of Iran, Mr. Ameer Saeed Iravani, is visiting India at the invitation of Deputy National Security Adviser Dr. Arvind Gupta from 22-24 July 2015.
The Indian and Iranian sides held delegation-level talks on 22 July 2015.
India and Iran Security Council structures have a regular dialogue channel.
Both sides exchanged views on regional and bilateral issues of mutual interest and agreed to continue their interaction in the future.
The Iranian delegation also met the Foreign Secretary Dr. S. Jaishankar and the National Security Adviser Shri Ajit Doval on 23 July 2015.

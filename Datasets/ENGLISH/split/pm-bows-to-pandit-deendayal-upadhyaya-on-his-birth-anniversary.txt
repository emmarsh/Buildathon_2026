The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has bowed to Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya, on his birth anniversary.
“A beacon of selfless service and an excellent organiser, I bow to our inspiration and guide, Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya on his birth anniversary.
Let us follow Deendayal ji’s path and create an India that is developed and just.
Have shared some thoughts on my Blog. http://nm4.in/1iPm6mO“, the Prime Minister said.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has greeted people on the auspicious occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi.
“गणपति बाप्पा मोरया! गणेश चतुर्थी के पावन पर्व पर हार्दिक शुभकामनायें।
Greetings on Ganesh Chaturthi.
‎On Ganesh Chaturthi we bow to Lord Ganesh.
May Lord Ganesh shower his blessings on us & enrich our lives with peace, joy & wisdom,” the Prime Minister said.

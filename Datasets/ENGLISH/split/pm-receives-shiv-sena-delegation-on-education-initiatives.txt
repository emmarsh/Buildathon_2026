Shri Aditya Thackeray, President of the ‘Yuva Sena,’ called on Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi today.
He was accompanied by a delegation comprising Shiv Sena MPs including Shri Anandrao Adsul.
Shri Aditya Thackeray briefed the Prime Minister on voluntary initiatives for the education sector – including distribution of study material for Classes 8th, 9th and 10th through “Tablet PCs” in the state of Maharashtra.
The Prime Minister appreciated the initiative and complimented Shri Aditya Thackeray for the same.

Mr. Hideaki Ohmura, Governor of Aichi Prefecture of Japan called on Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today.
Shri Narendra Modi fondly recalled his meetings with Mr. Ohmura in 2012 and 2014, and appreciated the close business ties between Aichi-based Japanese companies and India.
Governor Ohmura briefed Prime Minister Modi about his efforts to promote greater trade and investment relations between Japan and India.
The Prime Minister welcomed the initiatives taken by Japanese SMEs in this regard, and promised all possible support through the “Japan Plus” mechanism to their endeavours.
The Prime Minister also called for closer interactions between Indian states and Japanese prefectures to promote business to business ties.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has recalled Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya’s Mantra of Integral Humanism and contribution towards serving society, on his birth anniversary.
“We bow to our inspiration & guide, Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya ji on his birth anniversary.
His was a life dedicated towards serving society.
The Mantra of Integral Humanism, that was given by Deendayal ji remains a guiding force for all of us.
We are committed to fulfilling Deendayal ji’s vision of serving the very last person in the queue to build a developed & just India.” the Prime Minister said.

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, today invited all officials and staff of the Prime Minister’s Office, for high tea, on the occasion of completion of one year in office.
The Prime Minister said that the entire PMO family shares the credit for the success that has been achieved in serving people over the past year.
Shri Narendra Modi said that a sensitive and humane approach was needed in fulfilling the responsibilities of the Prime Minister’s Office.
The Prime Minister met various officials, and posed for group photographs with them.

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid homage to all those who lost their lives in Hiroshima bombings, during the World War-II.
“My homage to all those who lost their lives in Hiroshima.
The bombings remind us of the horrors of war and their effect on humanity.
Solution to problems of humanity lie in peace and progress.
Let us walk shoulder to shoulder to create a peaceful world, free from violence”, the Prime Minister said.

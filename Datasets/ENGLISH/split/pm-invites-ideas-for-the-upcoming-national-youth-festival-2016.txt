PM invites ideas for the upcoming National Youth Festival, 2016
The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has invited ideas from the young citizens of the country for the upcoming National Youth Festival, 2016.
The festival, which will be held in Chhattisgarh, will commence on 12th January 2016 on the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda.
“Young friends, what are your ideas for the 2016 National Youth Festival?
Share your ideas with me on the Mobile App. http://nm4.in/dnldapp.
The festival, which will commence on Swami Vivekananda’s birth anniversary on 12th Jan will be held in the beautiful state of Chhattisgarh,” the Prime Minister said.

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has spoken to Prime Minister of Nepal, Shri Sushil Koirala, on the situation arising due to yesterday’s tremors.
“PM Sushil Koirala and I had a telephonic conversation.
We reviewed the situation arising due to yesterday’s tremors.
I assured PM Koirala of all possible co-operation from our side in the wake of the earthquake”, the Prime Minister said.

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has saluted the security personnel who laid down their lives protecting others during the Mumbai terror attacks on this day in 2008, and reaffirmed India’s resolve to combat terrorism.
“We remember the horrific terror attacks in Mumbai on this day in 2008 and pay homage to the innocent men and women who lost their lives.
We salute the brave security personnel who laid down their lives to protect several lives on that fateful day.
They are our true heroes.
Today is a day to reaffirm our commitment to work together to combat the menace of terror and uproot it from the face of humankind,” the Prime Minister said.

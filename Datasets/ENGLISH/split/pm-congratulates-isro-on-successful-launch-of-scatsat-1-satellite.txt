The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has congratulated ISRO and its scientists on successful launch of PSLV-35 and advanced weather satellite SCSAT-1 and 7 other co-passenger satellites.
“Moment of immense joy & pride for India.
Congratulations to ISRO on successful launch of PSLV-C35/SCATSAT-1 & 7 co-passenger satellites.
Our space scientists keep scripting history.
Their innovative zeal has touched the lives of 125 crore Indians & made India proud worldwide”, the Prime Minister said.

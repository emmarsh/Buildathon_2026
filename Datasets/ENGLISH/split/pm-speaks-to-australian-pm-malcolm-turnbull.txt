Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, spoke to Mr. Malcolm Turnbull today and congratulated him on his appointment as the Prime Minister of Australia.
“Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull and I spoke a short while ago.
I congratulated him and conveyed best wishes.
I also thanked him for his birthday greetings.
I spoke to Mr. Tony Abbott and appreciated his efforts in improving ties between India and Australia,” the Prime Minister said.

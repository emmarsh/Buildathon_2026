A 70-member delegation from various states called on Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi today, to discuss issues related to the Jat community, including the recent Supreme Court decision regarding reservation.
The Prime Minister listened carefully to the issues raised by the delegation.
He said the Government is studying the Supreme Court decision and will try to find a solution to the issue within the legal framework.
The Prime Minister urged the delegation to take the lead in implementing the “Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao” Abhiyan.

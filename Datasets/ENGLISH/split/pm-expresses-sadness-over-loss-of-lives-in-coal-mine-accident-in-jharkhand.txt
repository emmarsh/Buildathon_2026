The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has expressed sadness over loss of lives in a coal mine accident in Jharkhand.
“Saddened by the loss of lives at a mine in Jharkhand.
My prayers are with those trapped inside.
Spoke to CM Raghubar Das on the situation.
Jharkhand Government & Minister @PiyushGoyal are working to restore normalcy. @NDRFHQ has been engaged for rescue & relief operations”, the Prime Minister said.

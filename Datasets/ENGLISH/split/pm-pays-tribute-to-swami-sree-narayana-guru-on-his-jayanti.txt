The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to social reformer Sree Narayana Guru on his Jayanti.
“I bow to the venerable Swami Sree Narayana Guru on his Jayanti.
His noble thoughts, teachings & fight against injustice always inspire”, the Prime Minister said.
Sree Narayana Guru was a social reformer from Kerala who led a reform movement against casteism and promoted new values of spiritual freedom and social equality.

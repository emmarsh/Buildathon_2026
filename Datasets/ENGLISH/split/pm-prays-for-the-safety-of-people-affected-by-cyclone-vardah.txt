The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has tweeted his prayers for all those people who are affected due to the adverse weather conditions caused by Cyclone Vardah.
The Prime Minister has also said that the Centre is working closely with the local administrations and army for safety of life and property of all those affected.
“My prayers are with all those people who are affected due to adverse weather conditions caused by #CycloneVardah.
Stay safe.
Centre, @ndmaindia is working closely with local administrations and army for safety of life and property”, the Prime Minister said.

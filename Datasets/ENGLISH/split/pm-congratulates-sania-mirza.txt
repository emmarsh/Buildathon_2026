The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has congratulated Sania Mirza for winning the US Open Mixed Doubles title.
“Congratulations to Sania Mirza for the victory in the US Open Mixed Doubles Finals.
We are very proud of the achievement,” the Prime Minister said.
The Prime Minister also congratulated Japanese tennis player Kei Nishikori for making it to the US Open Finals.
“Good news from US Open continued with Kei Nishikori`s exemplary win!
My best wishes to this young sportsman for the Final,” the Prime Minister said.

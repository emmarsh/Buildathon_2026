The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has greeted the people of India, on the historic occasion of 1st Constitution Day.
The Prime Minister saluted Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar for his contribution to Indian Constitution.
“Greetings to people of India on the historic occasion of 1st Constitution Day.
May this day inspire you to know more about our Constitution.
This day is a tribute to all those great women and men who worked tirelessly to give India a Constitution we are very proud of.
No mention of our Constitution is complete without remembering the stellar contribution of Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar.
I salute him.
Let us always uphold the ideals and values of our Constitution and create an India that would make our founding fathers very proud”, the Prime Minister said.

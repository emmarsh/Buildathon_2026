The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has condoled the passing away of freedom fighter, Shri Ghelubhai Naik.
“I condole the passing away of freedom fighter from Dang, Shri Ghelubhai Naik.
His was a life devoted to selfless service towards society.
Ghelubhai worked extensively among the poorest of the poor.
His efforts will be remembered by generations.
May his soul rest in peace,” the Prime Minister said.

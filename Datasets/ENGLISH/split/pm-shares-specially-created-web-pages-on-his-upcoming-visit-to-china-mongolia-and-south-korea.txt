Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has shared specially created web-pages, on his upcoming visit to China, Mongolia and South Korea.
“Express your views on my upcoming China visit on this specially created page. http://www.narendramodi.in/china_home.
Keep up with the latest updates on my Mongolia visit through this page. http://www.narendramodi.in/mongolia_home.
Here is a page in which you will find all latest news on my South Korea visit.
You can also share your views here. http://www.narendramodi.in/southkorea_home”, the Prime Minister said.

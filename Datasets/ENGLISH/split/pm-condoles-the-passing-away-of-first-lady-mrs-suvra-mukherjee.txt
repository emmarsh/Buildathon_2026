Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has condoled the passing away of First Lady, Mrs. Suvra Mukherjee.
“Pained to know about the passing away of Mrs. Suvra Mukherjee.
My condolences to Rashtrapati ji and his family in this hour of grief.
Mrs. Suvra Mukherjee will be remembered as a lover of art, culture and music.
Her warm nature endeared her to everyone she met,” the Prime Minister said.

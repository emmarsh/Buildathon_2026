Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has expressed grief on the passing of Shri Murli Deora.
“A dedicated leader, Shri Murli Deora’s warm nature made him popular across party lines.
News of his demise is saddening.
My deepest condolences to Shri Milind Deora and the Deora family on Shri Murli Deora’s demise.
May his soul rest in peace.
Just yesterday I had spoken to Shri Murli Deora’s family and asked about his health.
Very sad to hear the unfortunate news today,” the Prime Minister said.

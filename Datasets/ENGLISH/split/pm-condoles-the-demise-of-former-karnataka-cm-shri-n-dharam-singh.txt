The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of former Karnataka CM Shri N. Dharam Singh.
“Saddened by the demise of former Karnataka CM Shri N. Dharam Singh.
My thoughts are with his family & supporters in this sad hour.
Shri N. Dharam Singh had rich experience in Karnataka & national politics.
His dedicated service towards society will be remembered.” the Prime Minister said.

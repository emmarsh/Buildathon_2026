The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has condoled the loss of lives due to train accident in Amritsar.
“Extremely saddened by the train accident in Amritsar.
The tragedy is heart-wrenching.
My deepest condolences to the families of those who lost their loved ones and I pray that the injured recover quickly.
Have asked officials to provide immediate assistance that is required”, The the Prime Minister tweeted.

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has tweeted birthday greetings to Prime Minister of Japan, Mr. Shinzo Abe.
” I pray for Mr. Shinzo Abe’s long life, good health and wish him the best in leading Japan to new heights of progress ,” the Prime Minister said.
The Prime Minister went on to praise the leadership of Prime Minister Abe and emphasized both countries’ commitment to promoting Indo-Japan relations.
“Whenever I interact with him, I cannot but marvel at his insight and expertise on a wide range of issues, both local and international.
Both PM Abe and I are committed to taking India-Japan relations to the next level and contributing to global peace and development ,” the Prime Minister said.

On the 69th anniversary of the Hiroshima atomic bombing, the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid homage to those who lost their lives in the incident.
“Today we remember all those people who lost their lives in Hiroshima.
I hope humankind never sees such a day ever again.
Let us all work together and further the cause of world peace and make the world a happier and more peaceful place in the years to come,” the Prime Minister said.
The Prime Minister was also present in the Lok Sabha, when the House paid homage to the victims of Hiroshima, today.

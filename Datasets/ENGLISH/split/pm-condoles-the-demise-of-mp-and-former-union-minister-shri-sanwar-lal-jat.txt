The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has condoled the demise of MP and former Union Minister, Shri Sanwar Lal Jat.
“Anguished by the demise of MP and former Union Minister, Shri Sanwar Lal Jat.
This is a big loss for the BJP & the nation.
My condolences.
Shri Sanwar Lal Jat worked extensively for wellbeing of villages and farmers.
My thoughts are with his family & supporters in this sad hour”, the Prime Minister said.

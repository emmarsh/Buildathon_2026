PM’s remarks after visiting Tirumala temple at Tirupati
The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi today visited Tirumala temple at Tirupati in Andhra Pradesh.
After visiting the temple, Prime Minister said:
“May there be peace, unity and harmony in the country.
May we succeed in eliminating poverty.
May Lord Balaji bless the country for happiness and prosperity in the common man’s life.”

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid tributes to Loknayak Jayprakash Narayan on his birth anniversary.
“Loknayak Jayprakash Narayan was a true nationalist who devoted his life to service and nation building.
Tributes on his birth anniversary.
JP`s call for `Sampoorna Kranti` galvanised the entire nation and played a historic role in preserving India`s democratic fabric,” the Prime Minister said.

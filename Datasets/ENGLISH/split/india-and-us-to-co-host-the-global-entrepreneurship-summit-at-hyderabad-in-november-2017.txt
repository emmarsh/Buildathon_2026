PM says, GES is a unique opportunity for bringing together entrepreneurs and start ups with global leaders
India and US will be co-hosting the Global Entrepreneurship Summit (GES) at Hyderabad from 28-30 November, 2017.
In a series of tweets from his account the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi said:
“India and US will co-host the Global Entrepreneurship Summit at Hyderabad from 28-30 November 2017.
The Summit is a unique opportunity for bringing together entrepreneurs and start ups with global leaders.
Look forward to Ms. Ivanka Trump’s presence at Global Entrepreneurship Summit 2017-Hyderabad as the leader of the US delegation.”

Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid tributes to the first Prime Minister of India, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru on his 125th birth anniversary.
“Today we mark the 125th birth anniversary of our first Prime Minister Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru.
My tributes to him.
We remember Pandit Nehru’s efforts during the freedom struggle and his role as the first Prime Minister of India”, the Prime Minister said.

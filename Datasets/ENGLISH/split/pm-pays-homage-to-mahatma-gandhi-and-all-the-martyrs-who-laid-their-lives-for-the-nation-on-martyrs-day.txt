The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has paid homage to Mahatma Gandhi and all the Martyrs who laid their lives for the Nation, On Martyrs` Day.
“पूज्य बापू को उनकी पुण्यतिथि पर शत् शत् नमन।
My tributes to Pujya Bapu.
On Martyrs’ Day I pay homage to each and every Martyr who laid his or her life for our Nation.
Their bravery and courage will always inspire”, the Prime Minister said.

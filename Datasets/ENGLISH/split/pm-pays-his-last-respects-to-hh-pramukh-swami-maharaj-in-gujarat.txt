The Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi paid his last respects to HH Pramukh Swami Maharaj in Sarangpur, Gujarat.
“HH Pramukh Swami Maharaj was not only a Saint but also a Lok Sangrahak.
I pay my tributes to HH Pramukh Swami Maharaj.
May he continue to guide us and give us strength ”, the Prime Minister said.

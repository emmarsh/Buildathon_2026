The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid tributes to Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia on his birth anniversary.
“On his birth anniversary, I pay my tributes to Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia, a thinker par excellence who left a deep impact on Indian politics.
Among many other things, Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia’s commitment towards cleanliness continues to inspire us,” the Prime Minister said.

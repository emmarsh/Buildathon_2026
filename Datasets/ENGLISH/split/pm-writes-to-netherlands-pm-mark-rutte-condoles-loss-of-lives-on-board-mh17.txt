The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has written to the Prime Minister of Netherlands, Mr. Mark Rutte, expressing his condolences on the loss of lives on board MH17.
Shri Narendra Modi wrote that the people of India share the sorrow, and stand in mourning with the bereaved families and the Dutch people.
The Prime Minister wrote that he hopes the circumstances of the disaster are established quickly and that such tragedies are prevented.

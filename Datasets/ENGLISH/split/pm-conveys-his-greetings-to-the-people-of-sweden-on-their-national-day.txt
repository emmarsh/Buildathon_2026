Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has conveyed his greetings to the people of Sweden, on their National Day.
“On their National Day, I convey my greetings to the people of Sweden, a Nation that is India’s valued friend, connected by shared values.
There is great potential in India-Sweden ties.
Rashtrapati ji’s very recent visit to Sweden will strengthen our relations even further”, the Prime Minister said.

The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi, has paid tribute to the legendary hockey player, Major Dhyan Chand, on National Sports Day.
“Our tributes to the legendary Major Dhyan Chand on National Sports Day.
His determination & dedication towards sports continues to inspire.
We salute all our sportsmen & women who have been enhancing the pride of the Tricolour by their exemplary performance on the field.
Congrats to the sportspersons who would be honoured with the various National Sports Awards today.
India is proud of them,” the Prime Minister said.
